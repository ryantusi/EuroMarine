About Us

Proudly serving India’s maritime sector with quality, speed, and precision.

Euro Marine Supply is your reliable partner for maritime logistics and ship supply solutions, ensuring vessels operating along India’s coasts and ports are fully equipped and mission-ready.

Our comprehensive inventory covers everything from safety gear and maintenance tools to provisions and personal protective equipment, making sure ships remain stocked, compliant, and fully operational in Indian waters.

Founded on the principles of trust and efficiency, Euro Marine Supply is powered by a dedicated team of professionals who understand the unique challenges of India’s maritime industry. Our network of dependable suppliers and logistics partners enables us to deliver high-quality products quickly and seamlessly to ports across the country, tailored precisely to your operational requirements.

At Euro Marine Supply, our mission is to strengthen India’s maritime sector by providing exceptional service and premium-quality products. We are committed to cultivating long-term partnerships with our clients by consistently surpassing expectations and adapting to the dynamic needs of the shipping industry. Count on us as your go-to source for all ship supply requirements, and experience service designed specifically for India’s maritime landscape.
